Drift

For time series drift calculations, this hub depends on

  • Jensen-Shannon Distance $J_{dist}$
  • Wasserstein Distance $\mathcal{W}$
wherein $$J_{dist} = \sqrt{J_{div}}$$ $J_{div}$ is the Jensen-Shannon Divergence, a method for determining the similarity of a pair of distributions. The similarity between a pair of distributions increases as $J_{dist} \rightarrow 0$. Note, for a pair of distributions $$J_{div} \in [0 \quad 1]$$ therefore $$J_{dist} \in [0 \quad 1]$$ The Wasserstein Distance is a distance measure [Part 6].

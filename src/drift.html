Drift





At present, the drift of each gauge's time series is quantified via the Jensen-Shannon Divergence $\in [0 \quad 1]$, and the Wasserstein Distance. Whilst the Jensen-Shannon Divergence is a measure of similarity between two distributions, the Wasserstein Distance is a distance measure (Part 6).

River Levels / Latest Warning Area: Predictions

The predictions vis-a-via the latest warning area. Each graph's title denotes Predictions: River Level Gauge Location/Catchment Area. Each river level measuring device is located within a SEPA (Scottish Environment Protection Agency) river gauge catchment area. The forecasts bands hint at our degree of uncertainty. The river level values are the values relative to Ordnance Datum, i.e., relative water level + gauge datum. The predictions can be substantially improved by including known predictive factors, using an ensemble of models, and much more; the different models investigated for this exercise can be the starting point of an ensemble.

For short-time-horizon decisions.
